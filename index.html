This is Creative Dreams

Welcome to Creative Dreams! We make your dreams come true! At least within the areas of recording, mixing and mastering! Creative Dreams is located in the northern parts of Sweden in a small society called Vindeln. We started our journey in the winter of 1994 with one clear mission, to deliver top quality recordings, mixing and mastering solutions to our customers. We have worked with customers and clients from all over the world with fantastic results! The reason behind our success is our ambition to always exceed our customers expectations and with our 30 years of experience you can just sit back and enjoy the ride! We look forward to work with you!
